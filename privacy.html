Location Data

KHEDME respects that location in Lebanon is sensitive and private.

We handle location as follows:

Job-scoped location only (Clients)

  • You may choose to share a specific location for a job (for example, your home or office).
  • Location is tied to a particular booking and is visible only to the Worker(s) you book or request.
  • You remain in control of when to share or update this location.

Approximate worker area (Workers)

  • Workers can define the general areas/neighborhoods where they are willing to work.
  • We use this to show relevant Workers to Clients (and not your exact address).
  • We do not continuously track your real-time GPS location in the background.
